## SEO Role of the Page

The homepage serves as GameLayer's primary brand and platform positioning page. It functions as:

1. **Brand Authority Hub:** Establishes GameLayer as a leading gamification platform
2. **Commercial Intent Capture:** Targets decision-makers evaluating gamification solutions
3. **Platform Overview:** Provides comprehensive platform positioning for all audience types
4. **Conversion Entry Point:** Guides visitors from awareness to consideration to action
